Some Things to do in the Twin Cities

Mall of America

With over 500 stores, 50 restaurants, and a theme park, the Mall of America, the largest shopping mall in the United States, is a great place to spend the day. [1]

With over 500 stores, 50 restaurants, and a theme park, the Mall of America, the largest shopping mall in the United States, is a great place to spend the day. [1] Visit mallofamerica.com for more information.

Minneapolis Sculpture Garden

Art lover? Come visit the Walker Art Center and enjoy a nice walk around the Sculpture Garden.

Art lover? Come visit the Walker Art Center and enjoy a nice walk around the Sculpture Garden. Visit https://walkerart.org/ for more details.

The Guthrie Theater

Want to see a play? Go to the Guthrie, one of the most renowned theaters in the country. Visit the Guthrie Theater website for show times.

Want to see a play? Go to the Guthrie, one of the most renowned theaters in the country. Visit guthrietheater.org for show times.
